TRIPLE GOOBER BERRY SUNDAE COCKTAIL (FAKE RECIPE):

Ingredients:

For the Blue Layer

  1. 1/2 cup blue raspberry lemonade (or any blue-colored drink)
  2. 1/4 cup lemon-lime soda
  3. 1 tablespoon blueberry syrup or blue curaçao
  4. Crushed ice

For the Red Layer

  1. 1/2 cup cranberry juice
  2. 1/4 cup orange juice
  3. 1 tablespoon grenadine syrup
  4. Crushed ice

For the Yellow Layer

  1. 1/2 cup pineapple juice
  2. 1/4 cup orange juice
  3. Crushed ice

Garnish

  1. Maraschino cherries
  2. Orange slices

Instructions:

  1. Start by preparing the blue layer. In a glass or clear plastic cup, add crushed ice.
  2. Carefully pour the blue raspberry lemonade over the ice, filling the glass about one-third full.
  3. Slowly pour in the lemon-lime soda to create a fizzy layer on top of the blue layer.
  4. Gently drizzle blueberry syrup or blue curaçao over the lemon-lime soda. It should create a gradient effect, with the darkest blue at the bottom and a lighter blue on top.
  5. Rinse out the glass or use a new one if you prefer a clean look for each layer.
  6. For the red layer, add crushed ice to the glass.
  7. Carefully pour cranberry juice over the ice, filling the glass about one-third full.
  8. Slowly pour in the orange juice on top of the cranberry juice.
  9. Gently drizzle grenadine syrup over the orange juice to create a gradient effect, with the darkest red at the bottom and a lighter red on top.
  10. Rinse out the glass again or use a new one.
  11. For the yellow layer, add crushed ice to the glass.
  12. Carefully pour pineapple juice over the ice, filling the glass about one-third full.
  13. Top it off with a splash of orange juice for a touch of citrus.
  14. Garnish your Triple Goober Berry Sunrise with a maraschino cherry on top and a slice of orange on the rim of the glass.
  15. Serve with a colorful straw and enjoy your whimsical and vibrant Triple Goober Berry Sunrise mocktail!

This drink is all about the fun and creativity, perfect for a SpongeBob-themed party or just to brighten up your day with its colorful layers and fruity flavors.
