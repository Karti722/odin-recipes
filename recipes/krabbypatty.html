THE ONLY KRABBY RECIPE YOU NEED (FAKE RECIPE):

Ingredients:

For the Patty

  1. 1/4 pound ground beef (80% lean)
  2. 1/4 pound ground pork
  3. 1/4 pound breadcrumbs
  4. 1 egg
  5. 1/4 cup chopped onion
  6. 1/4 cup chopped pickles
  7. 1/4 teaspoon salt
  8. 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  9. 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder

For Assembling

  1. 1 hamburger bun
  2. 1 slice of american cheese
  3. Lettuce leaves
  4. Sliced tomato
  5. ketchup
  6. Mustard
  7. Mayonnaise
  8. Sliced Pickles

Instructions:

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine the ground beef, ground pork, breadcrumbs, egg, chopped onion, chopped pickles, salt, black pepper, and garlic powder. Mix everything together until well combined.
  2. Divide the mixture into two equal portions and shape them into burger patties. Make sure they are slightly wider than the hamburger bun, as they will shrink when cooked.
  3. Heat a skillet or grill pan over medium-high heat and lightly grease it with cooking spray or oil.
  4. Place the burger patties on the hot skillet or grill pan and cook for about 3-4 minutes on each side, or until they are cooked to your preferred level of doneness.
  5. In the last minute of cooking, place a slice of American cheese on each patty and allow it to melt.
  6. While the patties are cooking, lightly toast the hamburger bun in the skillet or on another section of the grill.
  7. To assemble your Krabby Patty, spread a thin layer of ketchup on the bottom bun, followed by a squirt of mustard and a smear of mayonnaise.
  8. Place a lettuce leaf and a slice of tomato on top of the condiments.
  9. Add the cooked patty with melted cheese on top of the tomato.
  10. Finish by adding a few slices of pickles.
  11. Place the top bun on the assembled ingredients, and your Krabby Patty is ready to enjoy!

Feel free to customize your Krabby Patty with additional toppings or condiments to suit your taste. Serve it with your favorite side dishes, and you'll have a delicious meal inspired by the beloved SpongeBob SquarePants cartoon!
